Privacy Policy – StarFlight

Effective Date: February 11, 2026

StarFlight ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable laws.

1. Data Collection

StarFlight does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The App:

  • Does not require user registration
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or account information
  • Does not access contacts, location, camera, or microphone
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not use advertising frameworks

2. Local Data Storage

StarFlight stores limited app-related data locally on your device using Apple's built-in storage mechanisms (such as UserDefaults).

This may include:

  • Mission progress
  • Unlocked achievements
  • Selected spacecraft
  • Optional timer settings

This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to the developer
  • Is not shared with third parties
Deleting the app will remove all locally stored data.

3. Data Processing under GDPR

Because StarFlight does not collect or process personal data, most GDPR data subject rights (such as access, rectification, or deletion requests) are not applicable.

If you believe that personal data has been processed contrary to this statement, you may contact the developer (see Section 7).

4. Data Sharing

No personal data is shared with third parties.

StarFlight does not sell, rent, or trade any user data.

5. Children's Privacy

StarFlight does not knowingly collect personal data from children. Since no personal data is collected, the App is safe to use by users of all ages.

6. Data Security

All app-related data remains stored locally on the user's device and is protected by the security mechanisms of the iOS operating system.
