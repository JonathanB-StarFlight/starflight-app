Privacy Policy – StarFlight

Effective Date: February 11, 2026

StarFlight (hereinafter referred to as "the App") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of its users in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable data protection laws. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information the App processes, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

1. Scope of Application

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of StarFlight. By using the App, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described herein.

2. Data Collection and Usage

StarFlight does not collect, transmit, or store personal data. The App does not require registration, login, or personal identifiers. Specifically:

  • The App does not request or collect your name, email address, or any other identifying information.
  • No location data, contacts, or device identifiers are collected for personal tracking purposes.
  • The App does not employ third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.
  • Screen usage, timers, and gameplay activities remain fully local and are not transmitted externally.

3. Local Storage of Information

The App stores limited information locally on your device to support its core functionality. This includes:

  • Unlocked missions and achievements
  • Selected spacecraft
  • Timer settings configured by the user

All data remains exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to any server or third-party service. Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under the GDPR, StarFlight’s handling of local data is based on legitimate interest for the operation and enhancement of the App. No personal data is processed for advertising, marketing, or profiling purposes.

5. User Rights

Given that StarFlight does not process personal data, the rights provided under GDPR (including access, correction, deletion, restriction, and data portability) are largely inapplicable. Nevertheless, users may contact the developer for inquiries regarding data management or privacy compliance.

6. Data Security

All locally stored data is protected by the security mechanisms of the iOS operating system. No external transmission occurs, minimizing exposure to unauthorized access. Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their devices.

7. Data Sharing and Third Parties

StarFlight does not sell, rent, or trade any user data. No personal information is shared with third parties, except when legally required by authorities or in cases to protect the rights of the App or its users.

8. Children's Privacy

StarFlight does not knowingly collect or process personal data from children. Since no personal data is collected, the App is safe for use by minors under the supervision of a parent or guardian.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes in the App’s functionality or legal requirements. The current version will always be available on this page. Continued use of StarFlight constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
